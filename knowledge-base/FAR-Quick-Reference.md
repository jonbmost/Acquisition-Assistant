# Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Quick Reference

## Part 2 - Definitions of Words and Terms

**Acquisition**: The acquiring by contract with appropriated funds of supplies or services (including construction) by and for the use of the Federal Government through purchase or lease.

**Contracting Officer (CO)**: A person with the authority to enter into, administer, and/or terminate contracts and make related determinations and findings.

**Contracting Officer's Representative (COR)**: An individual designated and authorized in writing by the contracting officer to perform specific technical or administrative functions.

## Part 15 - Contracting by Negotiation

### 15.101 - Best Value Continuum
- Lowest Price Technically Acceptable (LPTA)
- Tradeoff Process (allows for higher prices for better technical solutions)

### Key Principles:
- Competition is encouraged
- Proposals are evaluated against stated criteria
- Awards are made to the offeror whose proposal provides the best value

## Part 39 - Information Technology (IT) Acquisition

### 39.103 - Modular Contracting
- Break large IT acquisitions into smaller increments
- Each increment should be useful and add value
- Reduces risk and allows for course corrections

## Agile Acquisition Best Practices

1. **Incremental Funding**: Fund work in iterations or sprints
2. **Firm-Fixed-Price per Iteration**: Reduces administrative burden
3. **Continuous Stakeholder Engagement**: Regular demos and feedback
4. **Quality Assurance Surveillance Plans (QASP)**: Focus on outcomes, not activities
5. **Evaluation Criteria**: Consider technical challenges, oral presentations, confidence ratings
